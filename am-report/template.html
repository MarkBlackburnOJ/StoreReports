          Stock health KPIs
          Out of stock (OOS)
          Status='04' AND SOH=0 AND DROS>0
          Active items with zero stock but positive demand. Action: reorder immediately. These are direct lost sales.
          Possible phantom
          Status='04' AND SOH>0 AND days_since_last_sold ≥ 60
          System shows stock but no sales in 60+ days — likely missing, miscounted, or in the wrong location. Action: physical count, audit shelf vs system.
          Low cover
          Status='04' AND SOH>0 AND 0 < SOH/DROS < 14
          Less than 2 weeks of stock at the current sales rate. Action: reorder soon — these are tomorrow's OOS.
          Healthy
          Status='04' AND 14 ≤ SOH/DROS < 180 AND not phantom
          Adequate stock cover, not excess, selling normally. The goal state. The portfolio health % is the share of active items in this state.
          Lost daily sales
          Σ DROS for OOS items
          Sum of daily sales velocity for items currently OOS — units/day of demand not being fulfilled. Use to prioritize OOS recovery by financial impact.
          Total SOH
          Σ SOH
          Total stock units across the portfolio. Higher isn't automatically better — pair with DROS to gauge cover.
          Discontinued + SOH
          Status≠'04' AND SOH>0
          Inactive items still holding stock — dead inventory tying up capital and shelf space. Action: liquidate, return to vendor, or reactivate the listing if demand exists. Note: only visible when the Status filter includes non-04 statuses.
          Movement (week-on-week)
          Resolved
          OOS at previous week AND not OOS now
          Items recovered this week. The rep got these back in stock — credit them.
          New OOS
          NOT OOS at previous week AND OOS now
          Items that slipped this week. Investigate: ordering miss, supplier delay, or unexpected demand spike.
          Persistent OOS
          OOS at previous week AND OOS now
          OOS for the entire reporting period — at least a full week. Action: escalate. Often a supplier or listing issue, not a rep issue.
          Reference terms
          SOH — Stock On Hand
          Total units of an item physically present in store, per the inventory system.
          DROS — Daily Rate of Sales
          Units sold per day, averaged over a recent window. The demand signal.
          DSC — Days of Stock Cover
          SOH ÷ DROS. How many days the current stock will last at the current sales rate.
          Status '04'
          Active / sellable item code. Other statuses are inactive (discontinued, suspended, etc.).
